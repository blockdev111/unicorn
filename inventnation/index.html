About InventNation

With up to $1 million in funding available from Christopher Obereder s investment firm, InventNation is designed to help innovators turn their ideas into scalable businesses. Christopher is committed to building the next unicorns in collaboration with these inventors— transforming visionary ideas into thriving companies.

InventNation is not just a competition—it’s a revolution in innovation. Hosted by Hacksmith Industries, the world’s largest innovation channel with over 15 million subscribers on YouTube, and Christopher Obereder, one of the leading investors globally, InventNation is on a mission to find the next generation of groundbreaking inventors who will change the world.

Supported by Kickstarter, the go to platform for launching innovative projects, InventNation offers inventors the chance to secure life changing investments, gain global exposure, and receive mentorship from industry leaders. Whether you re an engineer, entrepreneur, or creator, this is your moment to make your mark.

With Hacksmith Industries’ immense audience reach and Kickstarter backing, the show is projected to reach over 50 million people worldwide. It’s the perfect platform for showcasing your innovations and attracting the attention of top investors and consumers alike.

Planned for 2025, InventNation is set to become the largest inventor competition in the world. But we need YOU to make it happen. If enough people express interest, this show will launch, and your invention could be the one to take center stage.

Our Hosts

Hacksmith Industries

With over 15 million YouTube subscribers and a global fan base, Hacksmith Industries has redefined what it means to invent. Known for taking fictional ideas from movies, TV shows, and comics and turning them into real, working prototypes, Hacksmith leads the way in creative engineering and innovation. From creating iconic gadgets to developing groundbreaking tools, Hacksmith Industries embodies the spirit of invention.

Christopher Obereder

A renowned investor and entrepreneur, Christopher Obereder has made over 100 investments in high growth startups, some of which have gone on to become unicorns. Having successfully exited two companies, Christopher is passionate about empowering the next wave of innovators. Through his investment firm, he is prepared to invest up to $1 million into the most promising ideas from InventNation, with a vision to turn these ideas into the next billion dollar companies. His goal is to not only provide financial backing but also mentor inventors through their entrepreneurial journey, offering strategic guidance and business support.

What is InventNation?

InventNation is a global competition designed to discover and empower the world’s top inventors. We’re calling on creators, dreamers, and innovators from every corner of the globe to bring their ideas forward. From tech gadgets and sustainable solutions to health innovations and new consumer products, if you’ve got an invention that could change the world, we want to see it!

Inventors will compete for the chance to secure up to $1 million in funding, build prototypes, and pitch their ideas to a global audience. The ultimate goal: help build the next unicorns— companies valued at $1 billion or more.

With Hacksmith Industries and Kickstarter as partners, InventNation provides unparalleled exposure for inventors, with the potential to reach over 50 million people worldwide.

How It Works

Step 1: Express Your
Interest

We want to hear from you! If you’re an inventor with an exciting idea, let us know. Simply share your thoughts and excitement by filling out the interest form below. The more interest we receive, the sooner InventNation will become a reality!

Step 2: Application Phase
(Coming Soon)

Once we’ve gathered enough interest, the official application portal will open. You’ll be able to submit your invention idea along with a video pitch explaining your concept and how it could change the world.

Step 3: Community Voting
& Expert Selection

After the application phase, the global audience will vote for their favorite inventions. The top picks from the public, combined with expert selected projects, will advance to the next round.

Step 4: Prototype Development
& Mentorship

Inventors who make it through will work closely with Christopher Obereder to refine their inventions. We’ll help you build prototypes, perfect your designs, and prepare for the final pitch.

Step 5: The Grand Finale

In the final stage, inventors will pitch their fully realized prototypes to a panel of investors and experts. The winner(s) will receive up to $1M in investment from Christopher Obereder, as well as the opportunity to build their invention into a billion dollar company.
